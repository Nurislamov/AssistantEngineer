# Validation Position

AssistantEngineer is a standalone C# engineering calculation platform.

Calculation logic is implemented directly in AssistantEngineer.

Standards are the primary calculation basis, including EN ISO 52010, EN ISO 52016, EN 16798, EN 12831-3, EN 15316, and ISO 13370.

External tools may be used only for independent comparison workflows.

EnergyPlus-based external comparison processes and ASHRAE 140 / BESTEST-style validation anchors are preferred external validation references where applicable.

No full validation/equivalence claim is made unless numerical fixtures, tolerances, expected outputs, and CI gates exist and pass together.

Open-source projects may be reviewed for general research, but product code and public claims remain standard-based and tool-independent.
