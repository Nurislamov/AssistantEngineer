# Telegram Closed Beta Operator Limitation Card

## What The Bot Can Help With

- Provide deterministic guidance from the runtime diagnostic catalog.
- Ask for clarification when a code or context is ambiguous.
- Show safety, verification, and provenance boundaries.

## What The Bot Cannot Do

- It is closed beta only and not a final engineering authority.
- It cannot replace the exact service manual, qualified technician, measurement, or field verification.
- Manual-codebook, staging, and preview are not final diagnosis.
- Coverage is partial; runtime catalog only supplies final bot answers.

## How To Phrase A Request

Provide manufacturer, code, equipment family/series, equipment side, and display context when known. Do not include credentials, personal data, or confidential site details.

## Ambiguous Or Missing Answer

Stop at the clarification or unsupported response. Record the displayed context and verify it against the exact installed equipment and reviewed documentation.

## Unsafe Equipment Behavior

Stop field action and involve a qualified technician. Human verification is required before field action. Do not bypass protections or safety devices. The bot provides no electrical or refrigerant hazardous instructions.

## Record After Each Test

- Scenario and displayed code/context.
- Bot response status, safety boundary, and verification requirement.
- Pass/fail result and sanitized evidence reference.
- Any ambiguity, warning, or escalation.

## Known Limitations And Safety Boundary

- Closed beta only; not production or public release.
- Telegram and chat ID discovery are disabled by default outside approved activation/setup.
- No long polling, database/audit persistence, or external monitoring.
- Runtime catalog is the only final-answer source.
- Vendor manual coverage remains partial; human review remains mandatory.
